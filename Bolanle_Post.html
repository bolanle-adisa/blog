How my journey in the tech industry has been so far .

Prior to the start of my college education, I had zero knowledge about all the perks of being in the tech industry. I intended on have an Accounting major. I was privileged enough to join an advising center that enlightened me on the benefits having a Computer Science major could provide. This sparked my interest and I went on to do further research. I came across so many success stories of people with similar backgrounds as mine and I decided to start my journey, hopefully, I will become an inspiration to the younger generation like others were to me.
When I started my coding classes at school, it was hard catching up because it was like learning a whole different language but this challenge did not hold me back. The fear of failure motivated me to utilize resources that I never knew existed. I discovered websites that taught me how to code in c++ and JAVA. Those resources ensured my success in these classes.
